Frequently Asked Questions

  • Enrollment in our Free Courses is instant and requires only a simple registration on our learning platform. All course content is freely accessible. The goal is to provide quality tech education without financial barriers.
  • The fee covers the cost of professional, highly secure real-time proctoring services required to validate the authenticity of your certification. This rigorous process ensures our Corporate Partners trust your results, leading directly to paid internship opportunities.
  • The stipend offered is performance-based, typically ranging from ₹1,000 to ₹10,000 per month, depending on your assigned project and contributions during the internship. This is designed to reward skill and meritocracy.
  • We guarantee an opportunity for an interview and placement within our network of verified Corporate Partners upon successful completion and certification of the Final Exam. Selection is based on the partner company's final recruitment criteria.
  • If you do not achieve the minimum passing score (60%), you are allowed to retake the exam after a specified cooling period. We recommend reviewing the course material and attempting the **Practice Tests** before your next attempt. The exam fee applies to each attempt.
  • The AI Playground is a space to practice and refine your Prompt Engineering skills. You can find trending image generation prompts, large language model (LLM) templates, and guides to maximize creative and accurate output from AI tools like Gemini.
  • The **ATS (Applicant Tracking System)** checker analyzes your resume against industry standards and job descriptions. By boosting your ATS score, you ensure your resume passes initial screening software and is seen by human recruiters for IT internship applications.
