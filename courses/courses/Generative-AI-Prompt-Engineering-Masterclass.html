Generative AI & Prompt Engineering Masterclass

Master the new language of AI. Learn to craft precision prompts to extract maximum value, creativity, and accuracy from large language models (LLMs).

4.7 Rating (5K+ Enrollments) Est. Time: 1 to 1.5 Hours Certificate Included

Course Overview

Prompt Engineering is the most in-demand skill in the AI world. This course focuses on practical, repeatable techniques to guide LLMs like Gemini and ChatGPT. You'll learn to move beyond basic questions to creating complex, multi-step instructions, ensuring professional-grade outputs every time. This is key training for any AI Strategist or Prompt Engineer role.

What You Will Learn (Key Topics)

  • Zero-shot & Few-shot Prompting: Techniques for structuring prompts .
  • Role and Persona Injection: Defining AI roles for specialized, expert outputs.
  • Chain-of-Thought (CoT) Prompting: Using reasoning to improve AI logic and problem-solving accuracy.
  • Controlling Output Parameters: Specifying format, tone, style, and length for consistent results.
  • Generative Image Prompting: Master descriptive keywords for DALL-E and Midjourney-style models.

Prerequisites Before Starting

This course is designed for rapid skill acquisition. We recommend you have:

  • Basic computer literacy and familiarity with browser-based applications.
  • An understanding of what large language models (LLMs) are.
  • A strong focus on **critical thinking and clear communication**.
  • **No prior coding knowledge** or advanced technical background is required.
