Privacy Policy

Your trust is our top priority. We are transparent about how we collect and use your data.

This Privacy Policy describes how Internadda ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your personal information when you use our website, courses, and internship services (collectively, the "Service").

Last Updated: November 8, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information primarily to provide and improve our services, communicate with you, and connect you with internship opportunities.

A. Information You Provide Directly

When you use our Service, you may provide us with the following personal information:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, and phone number (used for communication, support, and placement contact).
  • Educational/Career Data: Academic background, skill assessment scores, resume details (if you use our tools), and internship preferences (used for screening and matching with corporate partners).
  • Payment Information: We do not store full payment card details. Payment processing is handled by secure, third-party payment gateways (e.g., Razorpay, Stripe). We only retain transaction records.

B. Information Collected Automatically (Cookies and Tracking)

We use standard technologies to automatically collect information about your usage of the Service:

  • Usage Data: Information about your interactions, such as courses viewed, lessons completed, time spent on pages, and buttons clicked (used to track progress and improve content).
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers (used for security and troubleshooting).
  • Cookies: Small data files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences, keep you logged into the session (although login is removed, session management may still occur for progress tracking), and analyze site traffic.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  1. Service Provision: To provide access to free courses, manage your progress, and issue certificates.
  2. Internship Placement: To match your profile, resume data, and skill test scores with our corporate partners for paid internship opportunities.
  3. Communication: To send you essential service updates, educational content, career newsletters, and respond to your support requests.
  4. Improvement and Analytics: To understand user behavior, troubleshoot technical issues, and improve the quality and relevance of our course materials and platform features.
  5. Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property, and that of our affiliates, partners, and users.

3. Sharing Your Information

We only share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Corporate Partners: If you successfully pass a Final Exam, we will share your **name, contact information, and skill assessment scores/resume** with relevant corporate partners for the sole purpose of internship placement.
  • Service Providers: We use third-party companies and individuals to facilitate our Service (e.g., cloud hosting, payment processing, analytics). These providers only have access to the necessary information to perform their tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a legal obligation or protect the rights or safety of Internadda or the public.

4. Data Security and Retention

We employ commercially acceptable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide you with the Service or for other essential purposes such as complying with our legal obligations, resolving disputes, and enforcing our agreements.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

Email: support@internadda.com

By using our Service, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

Your Career is Secure With Us

We handle your data responsibly so you can focus on **learning, interning, and earning**.
