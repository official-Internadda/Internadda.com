Our Journey

From a Dorm Room to a Movement

Internadda started with a simple observation: students were graduating with degrees but without the practical skills companies needed. We saw the gap between academia and industry firsthand.

What began as a small WhatsApp group sharing internship updates has now grown into a comprehensive Ed-Tech platform trusted by thousands. We believe that education should be accessible and internships should be paid—that's the core philosophy driving every decision we make.

Today, we're proud to be creating real impact in students' lives, helping them gain financial independence while building valuable career skills.
