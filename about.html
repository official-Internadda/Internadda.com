Our Journey

From a Dorm Room to a Movement

Internadda started with a simple observation: students were graduating with degrees but without the practical skills companies needed.

What began as a small WhatsApp group sharing internship updates has now grown into a comprehensive Ed-Tech platform. We believe that education should be free and internships should be paid. That's the core philosophy that drives every decision we make.
