Our Mission and Story

Connecting India's youth with real-world experience and opportunity.

Bridging the Skill Gap

Founded on the principle that practical skills and real-world experience are the true currency of the job market, Internadda was created to solve the massive skill gap faced by Indian students and graduates. We realized that traditional education often falls short in preparing students for industry demands.

The Learn, Intern, Earn Model

Our unique model is simple and highly effective: Learn through free, high-quality, industry-relevant courses; Intern by passing our rigorous skill tests to unlock paid internships with our top partner companies; and Earn a strong start to your professional career. We are committed to making education accessible and employment attainable for everyone.
