Our Journey

From WhatsApp Group to Educational Movement

It all started with a simple realization: students were graduating with degrees but without the practical skills that companies actually needed. The gap between academia and industry was widening, and traditional education wasn't keeping pace.

What began as a small WhatsApp group sharing internship opportunities among friends has grown into India's fastest-growing student career platform. We started by helping just 50 students find meaningful internships.

"Our mission is clear: quality education should be free, and real-world experience should be paid. This isn't just a business model—it's our commitment to India's youth."
