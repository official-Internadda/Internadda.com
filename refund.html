Refund Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

1. Overview

At Internadda, we are committed to providing high-quality educational content and internship opportunities. We want you to be satisfied with your purchase. However, due to the digital nature of our products and services, we have a specific refund policy outlined below.

2. Online Courses

Most of our courses are offered free of charge. For any paid courses or certification programs:

  • Refund requests must be made within 7 days of purchase.
  • A refund will only be issued if less than 20% of the course content has been accessed or completed.
  • Once a certificate has been issued, no refunds will be granted.

3. Internship Programs

Fees paid for guaranteed internship programs or premium placement services are generally non-refundable, except in the following cases:

  • If Internadda fails to provide the promised internship opportunity within the specified timeframe.
  • If the program is cancelled by Internadda before the start date.

4. Non-Refundable Items

The following items are strictly non-refundable:

  • Resume building services once the first draft has been delivered.
  • Personalized career coaching sessions that have already taken place.
  • Administrative fees or registration charges.

5. Processing of Refunds

Approved refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days to the original payment method. Please note that your bank or credit card issuer may take additional time to post the refund to your account.

Need Assistance?

If you believe you are eligible for a refund or have questions about a charge, please contact our support team at payments@internadda.com
