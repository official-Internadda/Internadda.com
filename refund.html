Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: October 16, 2025

1. Course Fees

All courses currently listed on Internadda are free of charge. Therefore, no refund policy is applicable to course enrollments.

2. Internship Test Fees

If a fee is charged for any final internship examination, it will be clearly stated before payment. This fee is non-refundable as it covers the administrative costs of conducting the test.

You may delete your account at any time from your profile settings. As our services are free, no cancellation fees apply.
